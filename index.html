Why KnowBridge?

Many people see academia as a distant world, speaking a language few understand. Academic knowledge shapes our lives every day — yet much of it stays locked behind technical language, paywalls, and institutional barriers.

KnowBridge exists to help knowledge travel further — connecting research with public life through multilingual storytelling, videos, podcasts, and accessible digital content.

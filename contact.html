Frequently asked

Is KnowBridge free to use?

Yes. Everything we publish — videos, podcasts, and stories — is free and open. Access is the whole point.

Can I suggest a topic or research story?

Please do. Use the form above and choose "Suggest a topic." We read every message.

Do you offer content in other languages?

We're building toward English, Arabic, French, and Spanish. Multilingual access is central to our mission.

Can KnowBridge work with my school or organisation?

Absolutely — choose "For educators" or "Collaboration" in the form and tell us what you have in mind.

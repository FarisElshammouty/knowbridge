About KnowBridge

Knowledge belongs to everyone.

KnowBridge connects academic research with public life — through multilingual videos, podcasts, and stories that make complex ideas welcoming.

Home/About

Our Mission

Academic knowledge shapes our lives every day — how we stay healthy, how we understand the past, how we make decisions about the future. Yet much of it stays locked behind technical language, paywalls, and institutional walls.

KnowBridge exists to help that knowledge travel further: out of the journals and lecture halls, and into everyday conversation.

We translate research into clear, human stories — and we do it in more than one language, because curiosity has no single mother tongue.
